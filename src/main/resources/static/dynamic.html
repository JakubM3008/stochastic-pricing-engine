Almgren-Chriss Parameters Help Documentation [CLOSE X]

TOTAL SHARES: The absolute size of the position to liquidate during the time window.

INITIAL PRICE: Initial mid-market price of the asset in USD.

VOLATILITY PER STEP: Forecasted asset returns standard deviation per interval step.

RISK AVERSION (λ): Controls trade-off between execution cost and market volatility risk. High values accelerate trade execution to secure capital, whereas low values delay execution to avoid market impact costs.

AVERAGE DAILY VOLUME (ADV): Total shares of the asset traded daily in the market. Used in the Square-Root Law to compute impact.

IMPACT SCALING (Y): Historical calibration scalar (normally between 0.5 and 0.7) for market impact.

PERMANENT FRACTION (C): Permanent impact fraction coefficient scaling the permanent slippage rate.

LIQUIDATION TIME WINDOW: Total active trading session. Bins split the window into equal 30-minute intervals.
